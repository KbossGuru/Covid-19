STATEMENT OF THE PROBLEM - Identify the trends in Covid-19 in different Countries

SOLUTION 
- About 36% of the recorded cases recovered from Covid-19
- Only about 4% of the recorded cases led to deaths
- North American countries recorded the most cases and the most deaths
- Australian countries recorded the least cases and in return, the least amount of deaths
- European countries recorded the highest amount of tests
- USA recorded the highest amount of cases and deaths 
- Brazil recorded the highest amount of recovery 
- New Cases, deaths and recorded cases increased daily in 2020
